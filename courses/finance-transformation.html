Program overview

What this program covers.

Most finance teams are excellent at recording what happened. This program teaches you how to build the structures, processes, and tools that make finance a forward-looking, decision-enabling function — trusted by leadership and respected across the business.

Who this is for

👔 Finance managers and controllers looking to move up
📊 CFOs and finance directors redesigning their function
⚙️ Operations leaders who need to work effectively with finance
🔄 Transformation leads with finance in scope

What you will be able to do

✓

Diagnose your current finance function and identify the highest-impact improvement opportunities

✓

Design and implement a KPI and dashboard framework aligned to leadership decisions

✓

Apply IFRS and SOX standards in a practical, operationally relevant way

✓

Redesign budgeting and forecasting processes for speed and accuracy

✓

Identify and eliminate manual workload through process redesign and automation

✓

Build the business case for finance transformation initiatives
