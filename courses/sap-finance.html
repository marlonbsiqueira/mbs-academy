SAP S/4HANA for finance practitioners — covering R2R, P2P and OTC end-to-end, financial reporting, post-go-live optimisation, and Power BI integration. Built by someone who led UAT and post-go-live optimisation across 15+ European legal entities and still operates in the system daily.

Program overview

What this program covers.

Most SAP training is either too technical (written for consultants) or too high-level (written for managers who never touch the system). This program sits at the intersection: it is written for finance professionals who need to work effectively in S/4HANA, understand what it can and cannot do, extract and use financial data, and participate meaningfully in implementation and optimisation conversations.

Every module draws on active experience: leading UAT for a 15+ entity European SAP S/4HANA implementation, driving post-go-live optimisation, building the Power BI integration that replaced SAP standard reporting for 70+ leaders, and resolving the real-world gaps that appear after go-live.

Program modules

01 SAP S/4HANA Finance — Architecture and Navigation for Practitioners ~85 min
S/4HANA vs ECC: what actually changed and why it matters for finance teams
The Universal Journal (ACDOCA): the core data structure in S/4HANA Finance
Navigation, transaction codes, and Fiori apps for finance
Chart of accounts, cost centres, profit centres, and controlling areas — how they work together
The 15-entity structure: how multi-entity SAP configurations differ from single-entity
02 Record-to-Report (R2R) in SAP S/4HANA ~110 min
General ledger posting, journal entries, and clearing in S/4HANA
Month-end close activities: accruals, provisions, intercompany reconciliations
The automated close checklist that reduced month-end close from 8 to 6 days in a 15-entity SSC/GBS
Consolidation and group reporting in S/4HANA
IFRS vs GAAP handling in the Universal Journal
03 Purchase-to-Pay (P2P) in SAP S/4HANA ~95 min
The P2P cycle end-to-end: requisition, PO, GR, invoice, payment
3-way match in SAP: how it works and what breaks it
Accounts payable management and vendor reconciliation
SAP Ariba integration and procurement controls for SOX compliance
Common P2P bottlenecks in multi-entity environments and how to resolve them
04 Order-to-Cash (OTC) in SAP S/4HANA ~90 min
The OTC cycle: order, delivery, billing, AR, cash application
Credit management and customer risk in S/4HANA
Accounts receivable management: ageing analysis, dunning, collections
Revenue recognition under IFRS 15 in S/4HANA
Intercompany billing and reconciliation across legal entities
05 SAP Financial Reporting and Data Extraction ~100 min
SAP standard financial reports: P&L, balance sheet, cash flow in S/4HANA
Flexible GL reporting: line items, trial balance, comparative analysis
Data extraction techniques: ALV reports, SAP Query, custom extracts
Connecting SAP data to Excel and Power BI for management reporting
The reporting architecture built in a 15-entity European SSC: from SAP raw data to 70+ leader dashboards
06 SAP + Power BI Integration ~105 min
Connection patterns: OData, SAP BW, flat file extract, direct connector
Structuring SAP data for Power BI: tables, relationships, and calculated columns
Building finance dashboards on top of SAP data: P&L, balance sheet, cash position, KPIs
Automating the data refresh cycle: scheduled extract and push to Power BI
The dashboard architecture that replaced SAP standard reporting for 70+ senior leaders across Europe
07 Post-Go-Live Optimisation and Common Failure Patterns ~95 min
Why SAP go-lives often fail to deliver the promised benefits — and what to do about it
The 15 most common post-go-live finance gaps (from real 15-entity SSC/GBS experience)
Change management for finance teams post-go-live: adoption, training, process alignment
How to build the post-go-live optimisation roadmap — prioritising effort vs impact
UAT planning and execution: what to test, how to test it, how to escalate
Built from 15+ entity implementation experience
