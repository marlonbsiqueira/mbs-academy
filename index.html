Why MBS Academy

Built differently. Delivered differently.

Most training asks you to trust theory. This asks you to compare it against real outcomes.

🏭

Built from live, ongoing practice

The instructor leads SAP S/4HANA transformation and intelligent automation at Stellantis's European SSC/GBS right now — spanning 15+ legal entities across 8+ countries. The material reflects what is working today, not what worked five years ago in a case study.

📐

Structured for immediate application

Every module ends with a framework, template, or checklist you can take into your next working day. The goal is not certification — it is capability. You should be able to apply what you learned within a week of completing any module.

📊

Grounded in specific, verifiable outcomes

Every case reference in these programs has a result attached: month-end close reduced from 8 to 6 days, 200,000-communication cross-border programme delivered with zero disruption, 30+ bottlenecks eliminated across Stellantis's R2R cycle. The numbers are real.

🤝

Finance AND technology in one place

Most finance training ignores the tools. Most tech training ignores the finance context. These programs integrate both — because that integration is exactly where most organisations fail and exactly where the highest-value opportunities live.

🌍

Multinational context throughout

The programs are built on experience across 5 countries and 15+ legal entities — covering IFRS, SOX, GDPR, and the complexity of operating across different regulatory regimes. If you work in a multinational environment, this is built for your reality.

⚡

Custom delivery for teams

Every program can be delivered as a custom workshop for your finance or operations team — adapted to your tools, your SAP version, your specific process challenges. The content is a starting point, not a constraint.
