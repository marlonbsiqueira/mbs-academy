Why MBS Academy

Built differently.

01

Live, ongoing practice

The instructor currently leads finance transformation and automation in one of Europe's most complex multinational SSC/GBS environments — spanning 15+ legal entities across 8+ countries. The material reflects what is working today, not what worked years ago in a case study.

02

Results, not theory

Every case reference in these programs has a result attached: close cycle reduced from 8 to 6 days, 30+ bottlenecks eliminated, €200K+ in productivity savings, 200K+ automated communications delivered with zero disruption. The numbers are real.

03

Immediate application

Every module ends with a framework, template, or checklist you can take into your next working day. The goal is not certification — it is capability. You should be able to apply what you learned within a week of completing any module.

04

Finance + Technology integration

Most finance training ignores the tools. Most tech training ignores the finance context. These programs integrate both — because that integration is where the highest-value opportunities live and where most organisations fail to deliver.

05

Breadth without superficiality

From DMAIC projects to SAP S/4HANA implementation to Excel VBA automation: the programs cover the full spectrum a finance transformation professional actually needs — not just one narrow discipline.

06

Custom delivery for teams

Every program can be delivered as a custom workshop for your team — adapted to your tools, your processes, and your specific challenges. The content is a starting point, not a constraint.
